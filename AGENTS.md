# Project Context: Raven Adversary

**What is Raven Adversary?**
Raven Adversary is a public-facing lead generation website and marketing "Walled Garden" for an institutional M&A AI due diligence service.
It targets investment bankers, M&A litigators, and institutional analysts.

**Core Principles & Constraints:**
1. **Public Filings Only:** The service exclusively uses public filings (SEC filings, proxies, S-4s, etc.) for its intelligence. There is no external private investigation.
2. **Lead Generation Focus:** The website is designed to establish presence, rank on Google for terms like "M&A Due Diligence AI", and collect initial interest (leads).
3. **No 'Preachy' Warnings About NDAs:** The user is fully aware of institutional realities, MNPI, and NDAs. The lead form asking for a CIK/Ticker and firm is intentionally designed just to gatekeep access and evaluate potential clientele. **Do not lecture the user about institutional M&A privacy regulations** or suggest that the form violates them. The actual intelligence product sits behind a secure, private dashboard (off-site/handled post-lead).
4. **Tone:** Simple, sophisticated, precise, and authoritative. "A traditional banker is boring, but now they use AI."

**Development Guidelines:**
- Maintain an ultra-fast, smooth, and highly reliable UI. 
- Use simple but striking UI components without over-animating.
- Ensure SEO tags are optimized for institutional M&A AI due diligence.
